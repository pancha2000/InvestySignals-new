How does the 8-Factor Engine work?
Our engine cross-references 8 different technical indicators (including RSI, MACD, EMA, and Bollinger Bands). A signal is only generated when multiple indicators align, resulting in a high Confluence Score.
Can I auto-trade these signals?
Currently, we do not offer auto-trading or API integration. You must execute the trades manually on your preferred exchange using the provided Entry, Stop Loss, and Take Profit levels.
