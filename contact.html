Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about InvestySignals.

Are the signals free to use? +
Yes! All signals and analysis tools are completely free. We believe in democratizing access to professional trading data. We sustain the platform through Google AdSense advertisements.
Are these signals financial advice? +
No. InvestySignals provides educational market analysis only. We are not licensed financial advisors. Always do your own research, manage your risk responsibly, and never invest more than you can afford to lose.
How does the 8-Factor Engine work? +
Our engine cross-references 8 different technical indicators including RSI, MACD, EMA, Bollinger Bands, and volume analysis. A signal is only generated when multiple indicators align, producing a high Confluence Score and increasing signal reliability.
Why do I see ads on the website? +
InvestySignals uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Ad revenue allows us to keep all our signals and tools completely free for users. You can learn more about how we handle advertising and cookies in our Privacy Policy.
How do I delete my account or data? +
You can request account deletion at any time by emailing us at investysignals@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request". We will process your request within 48 hours in accordance with our Privacy Policy.
Can I auto-trade these signals? +
Currently, we do not offer auto-trading or API integration. You must execute trades manually on your preferred exchange using the provided Entry, Stop Loss, and Take Profit levels.
