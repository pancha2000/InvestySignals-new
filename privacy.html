Privacy Policy

How we handle and protect your data.

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register for an account, we collect your display name and email address. Our authentication is handled securely via Firebase Auth. We do not store your passwords on our own servers. For paper trading, we store your mock trading history locally in your browser via localStorage.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to provide our services, maintain your account, and optionally send you service updates or priority signals if you are subscribed to a paid plan. Your data is not sold to third parties.

3. Advertising Policy

We do not currently use advertising on this platform.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use essential cookies to maintain your login session securely using JSON Web Tokens (JWT). We do not use third-party tracking cookies for targeted advertising.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for authentication, database, and hosting. Your email address is securely processed by Firebase in accordance with Google's privacy policies. Payments (if applicable) are processed securely by our payment partners, and we do not store full credit card details.

6. Data Security

All communication between your browser and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS. Your account details and session are protected with Firebase Auth's robust security measures.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. You can manage your information from the Profile settings, or contact us at privacy@investysignals.store for full data deletion.

8. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting a notice on the platform or sending an email.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@investysignals.store.
