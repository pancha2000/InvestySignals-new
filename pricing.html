Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Free plan really free? +
Yes — completely free forever. No hidden fees, no credit card required. You get access to all basic signals and the full 16-factor analysis engine at no cost.
How do I upgrade or downgrade? +
You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your Profile page. Changes take effect immediately.
What is the win rate based on? +
Our 78% win rate is calculated across all published signals over the past 12 months. A win is defined as the signal reaching TP1 before the stop loss is triggered.
Are these Binance Futures signals? +
Yes. All signals are specifically designed for Binance USD-M Futures. Each signal includes leverage recommendation, entry zone, TP1, TP2, and stop loss levels with calculated R:R.
Do I need to connect my Binance account? +
No. Our platform is entirely educational. We provide signal analysis and paper trading — you execute trades manually on your own Binance account.
